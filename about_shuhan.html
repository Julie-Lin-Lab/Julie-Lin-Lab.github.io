Shuhan Shen

  • Sep 2021 - Sep 2022 M.Sc in Applied Genomics, Imperial College London, United Kingdom
  • Sep 2018 - Jun 2021 B.Sc in Biochemistry, Imperial College London, United Kingdom
    I am a PhD student currently working on a project focused on therapeutic hypothermia and its effects on RNA editing for neuroprotection. I have had the opportunity to study in Imperial College London, where my passion for neuroscience and research flourished. Thereafter, I gained one year of experience in industry, working on the application of metagenomics next generation sequencing. I am deeply interested in both wet lab experiments and dry lab analysis, allowing me to explore different aspects of scientific research. I am eager to collaborate with different researchers, share insights, and work towards our common goal of solving scientific problems in the field of neuroscience. Outside of the lab, I enjoy singing and practicing yoga as hobbies. These activities help me maintain a balance between my academic pursuits and personal well-being.
