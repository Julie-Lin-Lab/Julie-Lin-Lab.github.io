Zixin SUN

  • Sep 2020 - Jun 2023 M.Med. in Clinical Medicine, Chongqing University, China
  • Sep 2016 - Jun 2020 B.Sc. in Medical Laboratory Technology, Shanxi Medical University, China
    • I am currently pursuing a PhD, with my research focusing on the localized translation in neuronal axons. In the final year of my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate enough to secure a year-long internship at the Shanxi Academy of Medical Sciences. It was here that I delved into research on immune-related blood disorders, gaining invaluable clinical experience in the process. This experience laid a strong foundation for my subsequent academic journey at Chongqing University, where I pursued a Master's degree. During this time, I systematically developed my research skills and experimental techniques, sharpening my scientific expertise. My research interests span both wet and dry lab analyses, with a special focus on clinical disease studies. This dual approach not only allows me to explore the cutting-edge of scientific research but also to understand its practical applications. I particularly enjoy collaborating with peers and exchanging insights, as I believe these interactions are essential for tackling the complex challenges in neuroscience. Beyond the confines of the lab, I engage in various physical activities, including swimming, cycling, and badining. These activities are not just hobbies; they are vital for maintaining both my physical and mental well-being, helping me to keep a balanced lifestyle while navigating the demands of my academic pursuits.
