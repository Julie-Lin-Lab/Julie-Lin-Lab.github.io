Linkuo Shang

  • Sep 2017 - Jun 2021 B.Sc. in Biological Science of Honors Program, China Agricultural University, China
    • Currently, I'm an MPhil student specializing in the screening of marine lead compounds through iPSCs derived from urine or peripheral blood cells to induce i3-neurons. I got my BSc from China Agricultural University in 2021 and spent the next two years researching CRC and colitis, focusing on intestinal quiescent stem cells. This experience was followed by a year at the Beijing Genome Institute of CAS, where I delved into bladder cancer clustering using scRNA sequencing and CNV analysis. In the LIN lab, my focus has shifted towards the intriguing realm of neurobiology, a field ripe with complex and thought-provoking questions. The opportunity to delve into these mysteries and contribute to meaningful research is what drives my academic pursuits. Outside of my research, I enjoy discovering delicious foods and playing badminton. These activities help me relax and keep a balanced lifestyle.
