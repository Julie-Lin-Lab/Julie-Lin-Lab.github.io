    Born in Guangzhou, I spent the first 18 years of my life in this lovely city, from the kindergarten to the high school. Like many other Cantonese, I have enormous curiosity about life, the universe and everything. I spend most of my free time in arts, history and phlisophy. I once dreamed of being the first one from mainland China to publish a science fiction in Nature, but later I realized I could only be at most the third one to do so. So now my daydream has changed: maybe I can be the first talk show host with a PhD in biomed, or the first director making a movie for the life of the Generation Z in China, with also a PhD in biomed. So first things first, good luck with my PhD!
